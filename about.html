About Red Robins

Red Robins was founded in 1983 on the principle of serving great burgers. We keep things simple with local ingredients, a small menu, and one location. We're proud to serve the Seattle community.

We've been honored to receive the Made Up Burger Award from the makers of this website, as well as the Excellence in Fictional Fries Award.

Come in and try our out-of-this-world food today!
